How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation menu, fill in your information in the registration form, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How can I update my profile information?

After logging in, click on profil in the top navigation bar. Here you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage preferences.

Can I request a specific book to be added to the library?

Yes! We welcome book requests from our users. Please use the contact form at the bottom of the homepage with the subject "Book Request" and provide the title, author, and ISBN (if available). Our team will review your request and notify you if we add the book to our collection.

What genres of books are available?

We offer a wide variety of genres including:

  • Novels & Fiction
  • Science Fiction & Fantasy
  • Biographies & Memoirs
  • Educational & Academic
  • History & Politics
  • Health & Self-Help
  • Children's Books
  • And many more!
Browse through our categories to find something that interests you.

How do I borrow a book?

To borrow a book, you need to be logged in to your account. Browse through our collection, find a book you like, and click on the "Borrow" button on the book's detail page. The book will be added to your "My Books" section and will be available for the lending period.

Is there a limit to how many books I can borrow?

Yes, you can borrow up to 2 books at a time. Once you return any of these books, you can borrow additional ones. Premium members can borrow up to 10 books simultaneously.

How do I return a book?

Digital books are automatically returned after the lending period expires. If you wish to return a book early, go to our address you will find in the header section of our home page.

What devices can I use to read the books?

Our books are compatible with most devices including computers, tablets, smartphones, and e-readers that support common formats like EPUB, PDF, and MOBI. You can read directly in your browser or download books for offline reading using our mobile app.

The website is loading slowly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. If the problem persists, it might be due to high traffic or maintenance. Please try again later or contact our support team if the issue continues.

How secure is my personal information?

Your security is our priority. We use industry-standard encryption to protect all personal data. We never share your information with third parties without your consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Can I use the library on my mobile device?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works well on all mobile devices. For an enhanced experience, you can download our dedicated mobile app available for iOS and Android from the respective app stores.
